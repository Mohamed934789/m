The Prompt — Quick
Your goal is to write scroll-stopping opening hooks that make people unable to keep scrolling. Ask me only these 3 questions, one at a time: 1. What is the topic or product you're posting about? 2. Who is your target audience? (be specific) 3. What emotion do you want to trigger? (curiosity / urgency / fear of missing out / inspiration) Then generate 10 powerful hooks in these styles: — Bold claim — Surprising statistic — Relatable pain point — Provocative question — Controversial opinion Format each as: [Style] → "[Hook text]" Make every hook under 15 words. No fluff. No intros.
The Prompt — Advanced
Your goal is to create hooks so powerful they stop the scroll instantly and force the audience to keep reading — no matter the platform. Ask me these questions one by one and wait for each answer: 1. What is the topic, product, or message of your post? 2. Who is your exact target audience? (age, pain point, desire) 3. What platform is this for? (Instagram / TikTok / LinkedIn / Facebook) 4. What emotional trigger fits best? (urgency / curiosity / social proof / fear / aspiration) 5. What is the biggest misconception your audience has about this topic? 6. What transformation does your audience dream about? Then generate 15 hooks across these styles: — Bold claim: States something surprising as fact — Pain-point mirror: Reflects their exact frustration back at them — Surprising statistic: Real or hypothetical number that shocks — Provocative question: Makes them question what they believed — Controversial opinion: Disagrees with common advice — Social proof tease: Hints at results others got — Curiosity gap: Promises a reveal without giving it away For each hook: ✦ Write the hook (max 15 words) ✦ Name the emotional trigger used ✦ Rate virality potential: Low / Medium / High / Explosive At the end, highlight your Top 3 picks and explain why they will outperform the rest. Include emotional layers: urgency, curiosity, and social proof where relevant. Adapt tone and length to the platform specified.
